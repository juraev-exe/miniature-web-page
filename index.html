Nurturing Excellence, Inspiring Success

At Riverside Academy, we believe every student has the potential to achieve greatness. Our dedicated faculty, innovative programs, and supportive community create an environment where students thrive academically, socially, and personally. With over 35 years of educational excellence, we prepare students for success in higher education and beyond.

Our comprehensive curriculum combines rigorous academics with arts, athletics, and community service opportunities. We foster critical thinking, creativity, and character development in a safe and inclusive learning environment that celebrates diversity and encourages every student to reach their full potential.

Why Choose Riverside Academy?

Academic Excellence

Rigorous curriculum with AP courses, STEM programs, and college preparation that consistently ranks above state averages.

Small Class Sizes

Average class size of 18 students ensures personalized attention and meaningful teacher-student relationships.

Comprehensive Programs

Arts, athletics, robotics, debate team, and 20+ clubs provide opportunities for every interest and talent.
