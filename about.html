About Riverside Academy

Discover our mission, values, and the dedicated people who make excellence in education possible.

Our Mission

Riverside Academy is committed to providing a comprehensive educational experience that nurtures intellectual curiosity, character development, and leadership skills. We strive to create a supportive learning environment where every student can achieve their highest potential and become responsible global citizens.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in academics, character, and service, challenging students to exceed their own expectations.

Integrity

We foster honesty, ethical behavior, and moral courage in all aspects of school life and learning.

Innovation

We embrace creative thinking, technology integration, and progressive teaching methods to prepare students for the future.

Community

We build strong relationships among students, families, faculty, and the broader community to support student success.

Our History

1985 - The Foundation

Riverside Academy was founded by Dr. Margaret Thompson with a vision to create a school that would combine academic rigor with character development. Starting with just 120 students and 15 faculty members, the school opened its doors in a renovated historic building.

1990-2000 - Growth and Expansion

During this decade, Riverside Academy expanded its facilities and programs. The science wing was added in 1992, followed by the arts center in 1997. The school introduced its first Advanced Placement courses and established partnerships with local universities.

2001-2010 - Technology Integration

The new millennium brought a focus on technology integration. Computer labs were upgraded, interactive whiteboards were installed in every classroom, and the school launched its first online learning platforms. The library was transformed into a modern media center.

2011-2020 - Innovation and Excellence

This decade saw the introduction of STEM programs, maker spaces, and international exchange programs. The school achieved recognition as a National Blue Ribbon School and expanded its athletics program with new facilities and teams.

2021-Present - Building the Future

Today, Riverside Academy continues to evolve with cutting-edge facilities, innovative curriculum, and a commitment to preparing students for an ever-changing world. Our recent additions include a robotics lab, sustainability initiatives, and enhanced mental health support programs.
