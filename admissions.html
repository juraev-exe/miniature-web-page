Admissions

Join our community of learners and discover your potential at Riverside Academy.

Start Your Journey

We welcome students who are eager to learn, grow, and contribute to our vibrant school community. Our admissions process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our rigorous academic environment while contributing their unique talents and perspectives.

Admissions Process

1

Submit Online Application

Complete our comprehensive online application form. Include student information, academic history, and personal essays. Application deadline is January 15th for the following school year.

2

Submit Required Documents

Provide all necessary documentation including transcripts, teacher recommendations, and standardized test scores. Documents must be received within two weeks of application submission.

3

Schedule Campus Visit

Attend a campus tour and information session. Students and families can experience our facilities, meet faculty, and ask questions about our programs. Virtual tours are also available.

4

Complete Student Interview

Students participate in a 30-minute interview with our admissions team. This is an opportunity to discuss interests, goals, and fit with our school community. Interviews can be conducted in person or virtually.

5

Admissions Decision

Receive your admissions decision by March 15th. Accepted students have until April 15th to confirm enrollment and submit the enrollment deposit. Waitlisted students will be notified of any openings.
